Property Features

Our award-winning boutique hotel offers 7 lucky guest rooms, each with custom architecture, well designed furniture, and a private bath with awesome shower. Some rooms have luxury tubs.

Rooms 1 and 2 are located on the first floor in the original bungalow, with entrances off the lobby. The lobby is wheelchair accessible and ADA accomodations are available.

Rooms 3 through 7 are located on the second floor, with private entrances off of the common courtyard. A staircase is used to access these rooms. Some of the second-floor rooms feature private patios.

Public space includes the lobby, upstairs courtyard, and front porch and patio.

We offer guests the opportunity to sample some of the best of Austin's creative scene, with locally made furniture, art and artisan crafts throughout the property. Many of these items can be arranged for sale, allowing you to take home a truly meaningful souvenir. Please inquire.

We provide free off-street parking, free wifi, free bicycles and a host of other amenities. We are always looking to provide more fun stuff to make your stay in Austin truly unique.
