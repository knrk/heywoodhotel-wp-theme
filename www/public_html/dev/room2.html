Queen Deluxe

Located inside of the original bungalow on the first floor with entrance off the lobby. 279 square feet.

Luxury queen bed, loveseat, writing desk. Original longleaf pine hardwood floors, 28' tall ceiling with skylight, west-facing windows. Wet bar with Instahot filtered water for hot tea or French press coffee, and filtered chilled water. Refrigerator and minibar.

Luxury tub with incredible shower. 40” flat panel television, free in-room wifi, AC/heat, alarm clock, white noise machine, iron and ironing board, room safe, internet jukebox.

Non-smoking. Single or double occupancy only. No pets. Please note that we are unable to accommodate children under the age of 10.
