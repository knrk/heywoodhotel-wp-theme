The Nitty Gritty

Group Size

Full-property rentals for lodging can accommodate up to 15 guests. Standard group rentals for lodging can accommodate a maximum of 6 guests, to ensure the comfort of other guests staying with us. For private events, please inquire.


Rooms & Occupancy

The Heywood has 7 rooms. Each room has one king- or queen-sized luxury bed and can sleep a maximum of two guests. Room 7 has an additional sofa sleeper and can sleep a third guest. Each room has a private bath.


What’s Included

With a full property rental, your group will have exclusive use of our 7 guest rooms, plus lobby, outdoor courtyard and patio areas. Our concierge and housekeeping staff are on site daily from 7:00am to 7:00pm and are completely dedicated to your group’s needs. All rates include free parking, free wifi, free coffee/tea service, free concierge service and daily housekeeping, and VIP extras. We have free bikes that can be used by hotel guests during business hours and we can assist larger groups with renting additional bikes. There are no additional or hidden fees.


Cancellation & Change Policy

Your rental agreement will contain the full policies for your group reservation including the cancellation and change policy. For full-property rentals, 50% is typically required to reserve the property and the balance is due at the time your cancellation window closes (typically 60 or 90 days prior to arrival). Discounts are provided for payment by check or wire transfer.
